This ware reproduces the original signage displayed above the dispensary counter of the Hemlock Bay Apothecary during its period of formal operation under municipal licence. The legend — *Regulated Poisons* — is rendered in the Apothecary's customary typeface: composed in Cinzel, at a weight consistent with official notice.

The design is austere by intention. No ornament has been introduced that was not present in the source document. The border rule is original to the broadsheet format of the period. Issued as an open edition on unbleached archival stock, suitable for framing.

Prior to acquisition, each ware is inspected and found to conform to archival standards of legibility and composition.
